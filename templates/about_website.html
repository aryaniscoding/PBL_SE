How It Works

1

Vehicle Entry

When a vehicle enters the parking area, our ESP32-CAM captures an image of the license plate. The system automatically records the entry time and processes the plate number.

2

Plate Recognition

The captured image is sent to our server where the Plate Recognizer API extracts the license plate number. The system checks if the plate is registered in our database.

3

Parking Session

For registered users, the system creates a parking session. Unregistered plates are flagged for manual review by administrators.

4

Vehicle Exit

Upon exit, another image is captured. The system calculates the parking duration and automatically computes the fare based on time spent.

5

Payment & Records

Users can view their parking history and payments through their dashboard. Administrators have access to comprehensive reports and analytics.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the license plate recognition?

Our system uses state-of-the-art computer vision algorithms with an accuracy rate of over 95% under optimal conditions. Accuracy may vary based on lighting and plate visibility.

What happens if my license plate isn't recognized?

If our system fails to recognize your plate, our administrators will manually review the entry. You may be asked to provide your plate number at the exit for verification.

How is the parking fare calculated?

Fares are calculated based on the duration of your stay. We charge ₹20 for every full or partial hour of parking. The minimum charge is ₹20 for stays under 1 hour.

Can I register multiple vehicles to my account?

Currently, each account is linked to a single license plate. For multiple vehicles, please create separate accounts or contact our support team for enterprise solutions.

Is my payment information stored securely?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption for all sensitive data. Payment information is processed through secure payment gateways and we never store full credit card details.
